Game Guides & Tips

Explore helpful explanations and definations to better understand different game genres and mechanics.

What is Survival Game?

A survival game challenges players to gather resources, craft tools and to survive in an evironment filled with dangers, enemies, or hunger.

What is a RPG?

An RPG (Role Playing Game) allows players to develop a character, complete quests, and make choices that influence the story and gameplay.

What is an Open-World Game?

An open-world game gives players the freedom to explore large environments, complete missions in any order, and interact with the world at their own pace.
